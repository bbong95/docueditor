## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Uploading Documents
- Drag & drop a **PDF** or **Image** file into the main workspace.
- Watch the **[Developer Agent]** (Orange) jump into action to process your file.
- **Note**: Large PDF files may take a few seconds to split into high-quality images.

### 1.2 The Interface
- **Center Stage**: Your document pages.
- **Header**: Your team of AI Agents.
- **Left Sidebar**: Page navigation and history.
- **Right Sidebar**: Property panel for fine-tuning text styles.

---

## 2. Editing Text

### 2.1 AI Text Detection
- The system automatically detects text blocks using Gemini OCR.
- **Blue Boxes** indicate detected text areas.

### 2.2 Modifying Content
1. Click on any text box. The **[Designer Agent]** (Pink) will spin to acknowledge.
2. Edit the text in the Right Sidebar or directly if supported.
3. Adjust **Font**, **Size**, **Color**, and **Alignment**.
4. The system attempts to match the original style automatically.

---

## 3. Exporting & Quality Assurance

### 3.1 Downloading Results
- Click the **"Download PDF"** button in the header.
- The **[Architect Agent]** (Blue) and **[Analyst Agent]** (Purple) will supervise the process.

### 3.2 The Quality Report
- Once the export is complete, the **Analyst's Quality Report** will appear.
- Review the stats:
  - **Accuracy Score**: Precision of style matching.
  - **Change Log**: Number of edits made.
  - **Security Check**: Verified safe for distribution.

## 5. Troubleshooting

- **Text not detected?** Try zooming in or re-uploading a higher resolution image.
- **Download failed?** Check your internet connection (Google Fonts may be loading).
- **Agents idle?** They only work when you do! Start an action to see them move.
